<p><strong>The Self-actuated Temperature Regulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Operated</li><li>Pilot-Operated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-actuated temperature regulator market is segmented into two main types: direct-operated and pilot-operated regulators. Direct-operated regulators utilize a built-in mechanism that directly responds to temperature changes, providing quick and accurate control. In contrast, pilot-operated regulators rely on a pilot mechanism to indirectly control a larger valve, enabling more precise regulation over a wider range of temperatures and pressures. Both types serve essential roles in various applications, ensuring efficient temperature management in industrial processes.</p></p>
