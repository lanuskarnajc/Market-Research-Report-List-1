<p><p>The DC Signal Relays market is segmented into TH (Through-Hole) Mounting and SMT (Surface-Mount Technology) Mounting types. TH mounting relays are designed for insertion into PCBs, providing robust electrical connections and thermal stability, often preferred in industrial applications. In contrast, SMT mounting relays are compact and suitable for high-density circuit boards, offering automated assembly advantages and better performance in space-constrained environments. Each type caters to specific requirements based on installation methods and product application needs.</p></p>
