<p><ul><li>Embedded Switch</li><li>Surface Mount Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic door switch market includes two primary types: embedded switches and surface mount switches. Embedded switches are integrated within the door frame or hardware, offering a discreet solution that maintains aesthetics and reduces tampering risks. Conversely, surface mount switches are installed directly onto the exterior of doors or frames, facilitating easier installation and maintenance. Both types are essential for security systems, alarm setups, and access control, providing reliable detection of door openings and closings to enhance safety and monitoring.</p></p>
