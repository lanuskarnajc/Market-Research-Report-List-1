<p><p>The spray humidifier market is categorized into high-pressure and low-pressure systems, each serving distinct applications. High-pressure spray humidifiers utilize elevated pressure to produce finer mist particles, ensuring efficient moisture dispersion in large spaces, such as industrial settings or greenhouses. In contrast, low-pressure systems operate at lower pressure levels, generating larger droplets, which are more suitable for smaller areas like residential settings. Both types enhance humidity control, improve air quality, and contribute to comfort in various environments.</p></p>
