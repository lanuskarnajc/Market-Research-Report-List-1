<p><ul><li>Passive Leak Detector</li><li>Active Leak Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless water leak detectors are categorized into two main types: passive and active leak detectors. Passive leak detectors function by monitoring specific areas for moisture accumulation, alerting users when water presence is detected. These devices typically rely on sensors placed in vulnerable spots. In contrast, active leak detectors proactively detect leaks using advanced technology, such as ultrasonic sensors or flow monitoring, and can automatically shut off water supply to prevent damage, enhancing overall safety and efficiency in leak management.</p></p>
