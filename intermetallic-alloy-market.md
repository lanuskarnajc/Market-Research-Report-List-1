<p><p>The intermetallic alloy market can be categorized into four main types: Aluminium-based, Copper-based, Nickel-based, and Others. Aluminium-based alloys are known for their lightweight and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for aerospace applications. Copper-based alloys offer excellent electrical conductivity and are commonly used in electrical components. Nickel-based alloys boast high-temperature strength and oxidation resistance, suitable for gas turbine engines. The “Others” category includes specialized alloys that may combine elements from different metals for unique properties catering to niche applications.</p></p>
