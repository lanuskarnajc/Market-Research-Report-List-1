<p><ul><li>Direct System</li><li>Indirect System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Oil Lube System market comprises two main types: Direct and Indirect systems. Direct systems deliver oil directly to engine components, ensuring precise lubrication and reducing friction. These systems enhance efficiency and performance, making them popular in high-performance vehicles. In contrast, Indirect systems circulate oil through a network of passages and filters before reaching critical parts, offering broader coverage but potentially less precision. Both systems cater to different automotive needs, balancing factors like cost, maintenance, and performance.</p></p>
