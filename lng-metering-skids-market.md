<p><p>LNG metering skids encompass various technologies for accurate measurement of liquefied natural gas flow. Turbine flowmeters utilize rotating blades to gauge flow rates, offering high accuracy for moderate flow applications. Ultrasonic flowmeters employ sound waves to determine flow velocity, providing a non-intrusive option with minimal maintenance. Coriolis flowmeters measure mass flow based on vibrations in a curved tube, ensuring precision and density measurement. Other emerging technologies may include positive displacement and vortex flowmeters, catering to diverse operational needs in the LNG market.</p></p>
