<p><strong>The Polyurethane Based Thermal Conductive Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotropic</li><li>Anisotropic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane-based thermal conductive adhesives market is segmented into isotropic and anisotropic types. Isotropic adhesives conduct heat uniformly in all directions, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent thermal management. Anisotropic adhesives, on the other hand, provide enhanced thermal conductivity in specific orientations, making them suitable for applications that need targeted heat dissipation, such as in electronics. Each type serves distinct needs within industries like automotive, electronics, and aerospace, where efficient thermal management is essential for performance and reliability.</p></p>
