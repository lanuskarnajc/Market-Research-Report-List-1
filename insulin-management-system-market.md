<p><p>The insulin management system market is primarily categorized into three types: traditional insulin delivery systems, continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) systems, and hybrid closed-loop systems. Traditional systems include syringes and insulin pens, focusing on manual administration. CGM systems track glucose levels in real-time, enhancing self-management. Hybrid closed-loop systems integrate insulin delivery with continuous monitoring, automatically adjusting insulin based on glucose levels. These types cater to varying patient needs, from basic management to more advanced automated solutions, enhancing overall diabetes care.</p></p>
