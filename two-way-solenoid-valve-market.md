<p><p>The two-way solenoid valve market is categorized into three main types: Direct-acting Type, Sub-step Direct-acting Type, and Pilot-type. Direct-acting types operate directly under solenoid action, suitable for low-pressure applications. Sub-step Direct-acting types provide intermediate flow control, allowing for multiple flow levels. Pilot-type valves use a small pilot flow to control a larger flow, making them ideal for high-pressure systems. Each type serves unique applications based on pressure requirements and flow control needs, enhancing efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
