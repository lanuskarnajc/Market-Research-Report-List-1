<p><p>The laser marking equipment market consists of various types including fiber lasers, CO₂ lasers, and solid-state lasers. Fiber lasers utilize optical fibers doped with rare earth elements, offering high-speed marking on metals and plastics. CO₂ lasers use a gas mixture to effectively mark non-metal materials like wood and glass, known for their versatility. Solid-state lasers employ a solid gain medium, delivering precision marking, especially in industrial applications. Each type caters to specific materials and marking requirements, ensuring diverse market applications.</p></p>
