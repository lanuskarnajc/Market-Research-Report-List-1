<p><p>The automotive transmission market comprises various types: Manual Transmission (MT), Automatic Transmission (AT), Automated Manual Transmission (AMT), Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT), and Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT). MT offers driver control through gear selection, while AT provides seamless gear shifts without driver input. AMT combines manual control with automatic features. CVT allows infinite gear ratios for smooth acceleration, and DCT uses two clutches for rapid gear changes, enhancing performance. Each type caters to different driving preferences and vehicle applications.</p></p>
