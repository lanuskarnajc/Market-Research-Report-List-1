<p><p>The lubricating oil additives market comprises several key types that enhance oil performance. Dispersants prevent sludge and deposit formation, while detergents clean engine parts and reduce deposits. Anti-oxidants extend oil life by preventing oxidation, and anti-wear agents create a protective film to reduce friction and wear. Extreme pressure additives help oils withstand high pressure and temperature scenarios. Viscosity index improvers enhance the oil's viscosity stability across temperature changes, ensuring consistent performance in varying conditions.</p></p>
