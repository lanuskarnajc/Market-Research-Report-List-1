<p><ul><li>Main Distribution Boards</li><li>Emergency Distribution Boards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The distribution boards market comprises various types, including Main Distribution Boards (MDBs) and Emergency Distribution Boards (EDBs). MDBs serve as central hubs for electrical distribution, managing and directing power to circuits within a facility, ensuring efficient energy usage and load balancing. EDBs, on the other hand, are designed to maintain critical power supply during outages, providing electricity to essential services such as emergency lighting and safety systems. Both types are vital for safe and reliable electrical infrastructure in various applications.</p></p>
