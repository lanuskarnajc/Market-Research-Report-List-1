<p><p>The urethane casting resin market is primarily segmented into rigid and soft casting resins. Rigid casting resins are characterized by their high strength and durability, making them suitable for applications such as prototypes, molds, and industrial parts. In contrast, soft casting resins offer flexibility and rubber-like properties, ideal for creating products that require impact resistance and shock absorption, such as gaskets and seals. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods, addressing specific performance needs.</p></p>
