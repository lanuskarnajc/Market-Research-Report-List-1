The IEC60332 standards categorize the material's performance in flame resistance. IEC60332-1 assesses vertical flame spread for single insulated wires, IEC60332-2 focuses on flame propagation for vertical bundles of insulated wires, and IEC60332-3 evaluates flame spread in horizontal arrangements. Each standard ensures that PP products meet stringent safety requirements for various installation scenarios.</p></p>
