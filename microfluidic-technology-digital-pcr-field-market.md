<p><p>Microfluidic technology in the digital PCR field involves manipulating small fluid volumes for precise nucleic acid analysis. Two significant market types are microchamber PCR and continuous flow PCR. Microchamber PCR utilizes small, discrete chambers to isolate and amplify target DNA sequences, enabling highly sensitive detection. In contrast, continuous flow PCR allows for the constant movement of samples through a controlled channel, enhancing reaction efficiency and speed. Both methods contribute to advancements in diagnostics, research, and personalized medicine.</p></p>
