<p><p>The backup water system market is categorized into four main types: concrete tanks, metal tanks, plastic tanks, and fiberglass tanks. Concrete tanks are durable and ideal for large-scale storage but can be heavy and less portable. Metal tanks, often made of steel, are strong and resistant to environmental challenges, though they may corrode over time. Plastic tanks are lightweight and rust-resistant, making them easy to install, while fiberglass tanks offer excellent insulation and corrosion resistance, combining durability with lightweight properties.</p></p>
