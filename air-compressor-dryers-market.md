<p><strong>The Air Compressor Dryers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Refrigerated Air Compressor Dryer</li><li>Regenerative Air Compressor Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air compressor dryers market primarily includes refrigerated air compressor dryers and regenerative air compressor dryers. Refrigerated dryers cool the compressed air, causing moisture to condense and drain away, making them suitable for general industrial applications. Regenerative dryers, on the other hand, utilize a two-tower system with one tower drying the air while the other is regenerated, providing a more energy-efficient option for applications requiring very low dew points. Both types are essential for ensuring clean, moisture-free compressed air.</p></p>
