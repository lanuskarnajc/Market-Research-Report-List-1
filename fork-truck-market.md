<p><p>The forklift truck market is primarily segmented into two types: counterbalanced forklift trucks and warehouse forklift trucks. Counterbalanced forklift trucks are versatile and designed for outdoor and industrial applications, featuring weight at the rear to balance loads, making them suitable for various terrains. Conversely, warehouse forklift trucks are optimized for indoor use, offering narrow designs for maneuverability in tight spaces, including pallet jacks and order pickers, facilitating efficient storage and retrieval operations in warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
