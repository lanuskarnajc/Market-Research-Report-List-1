<p><strong>The Retinal Imaging Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OCT</li><li>Fundus Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Retinal imaging devices comprise various technologies, primarily Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) and Fundus Cameras. OCT utilizes light waves for high-resolution cross-sectional images of the retina, aiding in the diagnosis and monitoring of retinal diseases. In contrast, Fundus Cameras capture detailed photographs of the retina's surface, allowing for broader examination of conditions like diabetic retinopathy and glaucoma. Both technologies are essential in ophthalmology, offering critical insights into eye health and enabling timely interventions for various retinal disorders.</p></p>
