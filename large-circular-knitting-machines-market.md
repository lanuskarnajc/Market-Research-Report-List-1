<p><p>Large circular knitting machines are essential in the textile industry for producing various knitted fabrics. Single circular knitting machines utilize one set of needles to create fabric in a continuous tubular form, ideal for simple designs like jerseys and basic knitwear. In contrast, double circular knitting machines employ two sets of needles, enabling the production of more complex, multi-layered fabrics with enhanced patterns and textures. Both types cater to diverse applications, from fashion to technical textiles, meeting different market demands.</p></p>
