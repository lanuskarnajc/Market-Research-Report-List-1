<p><strong>The Reactor Vessel Internals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Upper Internals</li><li>Lower Internals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Reactor Vessel Internals (RVI) market is divided into two primary types: upper internals and lower internals. Upper internals encompass components situated at the top of the reactor, such as core support structures, guide tubes, and thermal insulation, playing a vital role in streamlining fluid flow and maintaining reactor stability. Lower internals consist of structures located at the bottom of the reactor, including support plates and core shrouds, crucial for managing coolant flow and enhancing reactor efficiency. Together, these components ensure safe and efficient reactor operation.</p></p>
