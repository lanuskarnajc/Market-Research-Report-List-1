<p><ul><li>LC Autosamplers</li><li>GC Autosamplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The autosamplers market is primarily divided into liquid chromatography (LC) autosamplers and gas chromatography (GC) autosamplers. LC autosamplers are used for automating the sample introduction process in liquid samples, enhancing throughput and precision in analytical laboratories. In contrast, GC autosamplers are designed for handling gaseous or volatile samples, offering automated injection into gas chromatographs. Both types facilitate increased efficiency, reproducibility, and reduced manual intervention in sample analysis, driving advancements in various research and industrial applications.</p></p>
