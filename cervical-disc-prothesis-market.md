<p><p>The cervical disc prosthesis market is categorized into three primary types: metal-on-metal, metal-on-polymer, and others. Metal-on-polymer options typically feature a metal core with a polymer outer layer, offering a balance of flexibility and resilience. The "others" category includes hybrid designs and innovative materials aimed at improving biocompatibility or enhancing performance in specific clinical scenarios, reflecting advancements in spinal implant technology.</p></p>
