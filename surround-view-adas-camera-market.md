<p><p>The Surround View Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) camera market is divided into two key types: monocular and binocular cameras. Monocular cameras use a single lens to capture a wide-angle view around the vehicle, offering basic surround visualization. In contrast, binocular cameras utilize two lenses to provide stereo vision, enabling depth perception and enhancing image accuracy. This distinction affects applications in parking assistance and collision avoidance, with binocular systems typically offering more robust features and improved safety metrics.</p></p>
