<p><p>The Endodontic Rotary Machine market features primarily two types: Electric and Pneumatic systems. Electric Endodontic Rotary Machines utilize electric motors to drive rotary files, offering precise control and consistent speed for root canal procedures. They are favored for their efficiency and ease of use. In contrast, Pneumatic Endodontic Rotary Machines operate using compressed air, providing a lightweight and portable option. While they may offer less torque compared to electric models, they remain a viable choice for specific clinical settings.</p></p>
