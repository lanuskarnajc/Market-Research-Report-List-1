<p><ul><li>Contact Voltage Tester</li><li>Non-contact Voltage Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage testers market consists of two primary types: contact and non-contact voltage testers. Contact voltage testers require physical contact with the electrical point to measure voltage levels, providing accurate readings but necessitating caution to avoid potential electric shocks. In contrast, non-contact voltage testers detect the presence of voltage from a safe distance, using electromagnetic fields, making them safer for quick checks. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring electrical safety and troubleshooting in various settings.</p></p>
