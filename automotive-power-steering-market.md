<p><strong>The Automotive Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electric Power Steering</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive power steering market comprises three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electric Power Steering (EPS), and Others. HPS utilizes hydraulic pressure to assist steering, providing a traditional driving feel but requiring engine power, which may reduce fuel efficiency. EPS, on the other hand, uses electric motors to provide steering assistance, enhancing fuel efficiency and allowing for variable steering responses. "Others" may include advanced systems such as Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) and steer-by-wire technologies, offering innovative solutions for modern vehicles.</p></p>
