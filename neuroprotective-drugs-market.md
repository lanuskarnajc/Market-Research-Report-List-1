<p><p>The neuroprotective drugs market is categorized into three main types: cholinesterase inhibitors, anti-inflammatory agents, and others. Cholinesterase inhibitors enhance cognitive function by preventing the breakdown of acetylcholine, beneficial in Alzheimer's treatment. Anti-inflammatory drugs focus on reducing neuroinflammation, which is linked to various neurodegenerative diseases. The "others" category includes antioxidants and neurotrophic factors that protect neurons from damage and support survival. Each type plays a crucial role in managing and mitigating the effects of neurological disorders.</p></p>
