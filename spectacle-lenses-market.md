<p><p>The spectacle lenses market consists of various types, including glass, polycarbonate, Trivex, and plastic lenses. Glass lenses offer excellent optical clarity and scratch resistance but are heavier and less impact-resistant. Polycarbonate lenses are lightweight, shatter-resistant, and ideal for safety glasses. Trivex lenses combine lightweight properties with high impact resistance and superior optical quality. Plastic lenses are versatile and affordable, available in various prescriptions. Each type meets distinct consumer needs based on performance, durability, and comfort.</p></p>
