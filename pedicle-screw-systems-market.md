<p><p>The pedicle screw systems market is segmented into three main types: conical, cylindrical, and dual-cored pedicle screws. Conical pedicle screws feature a tapered design for enhanced stability and ease of insertion, making them suitable for complex spine surgeries. Cylindrical pedicle screws offer a straightforward design with consistent mechanical properties, widely used in standard procedures. Dual-cored pedicle screws combine the benefits of both designs, providing better pull-out strength and flexibility, ideal for varying anatomical requirements and surgical techniques.</p></p>
