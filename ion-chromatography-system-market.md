<p><strong>The Ion Chromatography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Ion Exclusion Chromatography</li><li>Ion Pair Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ion chromatography system market consists of three main types. Ion exchange chromatography separates ions based on their charge, facilitating the analysis of anions and cations in various samples. Ion exclusion chromatography focuses on the separation of ions from neutral molecules, useful for specific applications like organic acid analysis. Ion pair chromatography employs counter-ions to enhance the separation of ionic and polar compounds, making it effective for complex mixtures. Each type serves distinct analytical needs in environmental, pharmaceutical, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
