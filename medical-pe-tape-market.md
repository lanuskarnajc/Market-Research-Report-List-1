<p><p>The Medical PE Tape market is categorized into two main types: Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is flexible, offering excellent conformability and is ideal for applications requiring gentle adhesive properties, such as securing medical dressings. In contrast, HDPE offers higher tensile strength and durability, making it suitable for applications needing greater support and resistance to moisture. Both types play crucial roles in the medical field, ensuring effective securement and protection in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
