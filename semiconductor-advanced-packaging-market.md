<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semiconductor Advanced Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging(FO WLP)</li><li>Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging(FI WLP)</li><li>Flip Chip(FC)</li><li>2.5D/3D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor advanced packaging market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP)** - Offers a larger I/O count and improved performance by placing chips directly on a substrate with a redistribution layer. </p><p>2. **Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP)** - Utilizes a smaller die size and features chips directly mounted on the substrate with minimal interconnects.</p><p>3. **Flip Chip (FC)** - Enables direct chip connection to substrates, enhancing signal integrity and thermal performance.</p><p>4. **2.5D/3D Packaging** - Involves stacking chips vertically or arranging them laterally for efficient interconnectivity, increasing space utilization and performance.</p></p>
