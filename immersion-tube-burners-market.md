<p><p>Immersion tube burners are used for heating fluids and can be categorized into three main types: gas burners, steam/hot water burners, and electric burners. Gas burners utilize natural gas or propane for efficient heating, making them popular for industrial applications. Steam/hot water burners leverage steam or heated water for temperature regulation, often used in process heating. Electric burners harness electrical energy for heating, offering precise control and lower emissions, making them suitable for smaller operations and environments where gas is impractical.</p></p>
