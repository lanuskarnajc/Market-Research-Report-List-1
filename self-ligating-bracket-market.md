<p><p>The self-ligating bracket market is categorized into three types: passive, active, and interactive. Passive self-ligating brackets allow wire movement with minimal friction, improving patient comfort and reducing treatment time. Active types provide enhanced wire engagement, facilitating more precise tooth movement for complex cases. Interactive brackets blend features of passive and active systems, allowing orthodontists to customize treatment according to patient needs, while still promoting efficiency. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to varying orthodontic treatment requirements.</p></p>
