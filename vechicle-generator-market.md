<p><ul><li>Direct Current Generator</li><li>Alternator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle generator market encompasses two primary types: direct current (DC) generators and alternators. DC generators produce a consistent voltage and are typically used in older vehicles, offering simpler designs but lower efficiency. In contrast, alternators generate alternating current, which is converted to DC for vehicle systems, providing higher efficiency and better performance at various speeds. The preference for alternators in modern vehicles stems from their ability to support higher electrical demands and improved reliability, driving their dominance in the market.</p></p>
