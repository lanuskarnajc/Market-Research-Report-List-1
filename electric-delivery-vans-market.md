<p><p>The electric delivery vans market primarily consists of two types: pure electric and plug-in hybrid. Pure electric vans are powered solely by electric batteries, offering zero emissions and lower operational costs, making them ideal for urban deliveries. In contrast, plug-in hybrid vans combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, providing flexibility by allowing longer ranges through gasoline use, while still benefiting from electric driving for short trips. Both types aim to reduce carbon footprints and enhance delivery efficiency.</p></p>
