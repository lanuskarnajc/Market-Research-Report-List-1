<p><p>The magnetorquer market encompasses three primary types: air-core magnetorquers, embedded coils, and torque rods. Air-core magnetorquers utilize coils without ferromagnetic cores, allowing for lightweight and efficient design, ideal for small satellites. Embedded coils integrate directly into spacecraft structures for optimized space utilization and reduced weight. Torque rods, featuring ferromagnetic materials, provide strong torque generation capabilities, suitable for larger applications. Each type serves distinct needs in satellite attitude control, balancing performance, weight, and integration considerations.</p></p>
