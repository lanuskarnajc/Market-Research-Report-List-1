<p><strong>The Marine Scrubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OpenLoopScrubbers</li><li>ClosedLoopScrubbers</li><li>HybridScrubbers</li><li>MembraneScrubbers</li><li>DryScrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine scrubber market comprises various types tailored to meet environmental regulations. Open loop scrubbers utilize seawater to neutralize sulfur oxides, discharging the treated water back into the ocean. Closed loop scrubbers recirculate a chemical solution for scrubbing, ensuring no discharge into the sea. Hybrid scrubbers can function as both open and closed systems, allowing flexibility. Membrane scrubbers employ advanced filtration technology, while dry scrubbers use a powder-based reagent for gas scrubbing without liquid waste, offering diverse solutions for compliance.</p></p>
