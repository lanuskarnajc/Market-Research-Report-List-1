<p><strong>The Surgical Navigation Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Surgical Navigation Equipment</li><li>Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surgical Navigation Equipment market comprises two primary types: Optical and Electromagnetic surgical navigation systems. Optical surgical navigation employs cameras and LED markers to track instruments and anatomy in real-time, providing high precision for various procedures, particularly in orthopedic and neurosurgery. In contrast, Electromagnetic surgical navigation utilizes magnetic fields to determine the position of instruments and tools, enabling flexible navigation in complex environments. Both types enhance surgical accuracy, improve patient outcomes, and facilitate minimally invasive techniques in modern medical practice.</p></p>
